How to use SubTrack

🚀 Getting Started

Click + Add New in the top bar to create a subscription. It appears in the Unplaced panel on the left.

Click any card to open the edit dialog — set its name, colour, monthly cost (AUD) and category.

📦 Building Your Stack

  • Drag a card from the Unplaced panel (left) into the Stack panel (centre).
  • Drag a card within the stack to reorder it — a blue line shows where it will land.
  • Drag a card back to the left panel to unstage it without deleting it.

🏷️ Categories

  • Essential Things you genuinely can’t live without.
  • Beloved You love them and they’re worth the cost.
  • Discretionary Nice to have but not essential.
  • Thin Ice ⚠️ On the chopping block — review and decide.

💾 Saving Your Data

⚠️ Your data is NOT saved automatically. If you close the tab without saving, everything is lost.

Save JSON downloads a backup file (subtrack-export.json) you can keep anywhere.

Load JSON reads a previously saved file and replaces the current state.

📊 Metrics & Filters

The right panel shows your monthly and annual totals, plus a breakdown by category. Numbers update live as you drag cards in or out.

Click a category pill to highlight all matching subscriptions in the stack — non-matching cards are dimmed. Click the same pill again to clear the filter.
